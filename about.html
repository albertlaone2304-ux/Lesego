About Glam Studio

Two passionate beauty artists. One powerful vision.

Our Story

Born From a Passion for Beauty

Glam Studio was born from the shared dream of two talented women — Rose and Losika — who believed that every person deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and celebrated.


What started as a small passion project in Gaborone quickly grew into a beloved beauty destination. From nail artistry to lash installations, makeup to frontal styling, the studio has built a reputation for quality, creativity and genuine care for every client.


Today, Glam Studio stands as one of Gaborone's trusted beauty studios, built on skill, heart and an unwavering commitment to making clients look and feel their absolute best.

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and celebrated beauty studio in Botswana — a place where every client leaves feeling more beautiful, confident and empowered than when they arrived.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional beauty services through skill, creativity and high-quality products, while creating a warm and welcoming environment where every client feels valued and special.

Our Values

Quality over quantity. Genuine care for every client. Continuous learning and growth. Celebrating beauty in all its forms.

Our Promise

The Talent Behind the Magic

Meet Our Team

Two specialists, one incredible studio. Get to know the artists who will transform your look.

R

Rose

Nail Artist & Frontal Specialist — Studio Manager

Rose is the creative force behind Glam Studio's nail artistry and frontal installations. As the Studio Manager, she ensures every client receives a premium, personalised experience. Her attention to detail and artistic eye make every set of nails a true work of art. Rose specialises in gel overlays, gel tips, intricate nail art, and flawless frontal installations.

L

Losika

Makeup Artist & Lash Technician — Studio Owner

Losika is the visionary owner of Glam Studio and the face behind every stunning makeup look and lash installation. With a deep love for enhancing natural beauty, she approaches every client with care and creativity. Losika specialises in full glam makeup, natural everyday looks, and classic to volume lash sets — always tailored to the individual.
