About Us

The story behind the studio

Meet Lesh

Welcome to Claws by Lesh — a nail studio born from a love of beauty, creativity, and the confidence that comes with a fresh set of nails.

Based in Tsholofelo West, Gaborone, Claws by Lesh offers professional gel nail services and intricate nail art to clients who appreciate quality and attention to detail.

Every client who sits down with us gets a personalised experience. We take our time to understand what you want, and we don't stop until your nails look exactly the way you envisioned them.

From everyday natural looks to bold statement nails — we do it all, with care and at prices that won't break the bank.
